(03) The maker

One studio. Over 15 years of experience.

Benjamin McLaughlin is an award-winning artist and designer in Vancouver, British Columbia. He trained at the Milwaukee Institute of Art & Design and holds a Bachelor of Industrial Design from Emily Carr University of Art + Design. With twenty years studying design and fifteen years in the industry, his studio practice spans furniture, art, and bespoke commissions for private and commercial clients.
