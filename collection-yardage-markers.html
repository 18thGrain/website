Yardage markers

Fairway and tee-side distance markers, commissioned for your course.

Walnut yardage marker with engraved distance, club crest and date mark.

Distance markers designed to match the material language of your course. Our A-frame fairway markers combine handcrafted timber construction with clear, colour-coded distance indicators. Available as fairway markers, tee-side markers, or both. Each set is designed as part of a complete course commission.

Our yardage markers are built from locally sourced hardwoods using CNC fabrication and hand-finishing techniques. The A-frame construction provides stability on the fairway while keeping the profile low and unobtrusive. Finishes range from natural timber tones to charred treatments, with engraved distance numbers, club branding, and colour-coded panels available as standard options.

Each set is designed to integrate with the visual language of your property. If you are commissioning tee markers or course signage alongside yardage markers, we develop a unified material palette across all pieces. Colour systems, wood species, and finish treatments are coordinated so that every element on the course reads as part of a single, intentional design.

Yardage markers are built for courses that take presentation seriously. Private clubs, resort properties, and facilities preparing for events or upgrades find the most value in a commissioned set that reflects their specific brand and grounds standards.

The commission process starts with understanding your course layout, distance system, and branding requirements. We develop marker concepts that address both function and aesthetics: clear distance identification for players, durable construction for maintenance teams, and a finished look that elevates the fairway. Sets typically cover standard yardage positions with options for additional markers at custom distances.

All markers are finished and inspected in our Vancouver studio before shipping. We deliver across North America and internationally.
