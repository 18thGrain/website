Course accessories

Range baskets, ball crates, and course furnishings. Designed for your property.

Range ball basket. Locally sourced hardwood with engraved club branding.

Range ball dispenser. Hexagonal form with gravity-fed ball delivery.

Course accessories designed and built to complement your property's identity. From range ball baskets and starter station furnishings to custom storage and display pieces, each accessory is commissioned to match the materials and aesthetic of your course.

Our accessories are crafted from locally sourced hardwoods using the same CNC fabrication and hand-finishing processes we apply to our tee markers and signage. Range ball baskets feature solid timber construction with engraved club branding, built to handle daily use on the driving range. Ball dispensers use a gravity-fed design in hexagonal or rectangular profiles, combining practical function with the same level of finish and detailing found across our full product line.

Accessories are designed to complement a broader course commission. When ordered alongside tee markers, yardage markers, or signage, every piece shares a coordinated material palette: matching wood species, finish treatments, and engraving style. Standalone accessory commissions are also available for courses looking to upgrade specific touchpoints without a full-property package.

This collection is suited to private clubs, resort courses, and practice facilities where the range and starter area are extensions of the course experience. Well-crafted accessories in these high-traffic areas signal attention to detail and reinforce the quality your members and guests expect.

The commission process follows the same approach as all our work. We start with a conversation about your needs, develop concepts that balance function with your course aesthetic, and move into production once materials and details are confirmed. Each piece is finished and inspected in our Vancouver studio before shipping.

We deliver across North America and internationally.
